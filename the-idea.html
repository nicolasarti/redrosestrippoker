The Idea

Nicolas Bellearti

Nicolas Bellearti's idea was born in 2013 initially as a simple photographic project, simple card / strip poker themed shoots designed to artistically emphasize the situation, ranging in a natural and playful way between fashion, portrait, glamor and artistic nude genres and is based on some famous photographs and paintings with clear references to the game of strip poker, mostly dating back to the fifties / sixties:

the models portrayed in these works convey a great playfulness as well as other strong contrasting emotions such as innocence, sometimes even embarrassment; mixed with the desire to provoke, wink and transgress by exhibiting one's beauty, simply with a mischievous look or smile as well as showing one's body, or part of it, or perhaps hiding, even modestly, nudity with typically glamorous techniques such as for example hand-bra all in a beautiful crescendo of contrasting emotions.
 
Another aspect that inspired the work of Nicolas Bellearti later, in 2020 and at closing the circle, are strip poker themed video games, nowadays there are many and with high resolution videos / images, but which at least Nicolas Bellearti's opinion has nothing to do with art; so much so that perhaps the products with a minimum of artistic content were the very first video games of this type dating back to the 80s, such as Samantha Fox Strip Poker https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samantha_Fox_Strip_Poker, released for the legendary Commodore 64:
The fact that the graphics were produced on such a limited machine made it a certain charm, somehow made the imagination work and somehow with Red Rose Strip Poker we want to merge the artistic aspect of the strip poker theme, of nude photography / glamor and wanting to self-produce a video game nowadays in ways that are partly comparable to what happened in the 80's. Red Rose Strip Poker looks to the future but with a retrospective perspective.
 
The result is what you can see and wants to try to stand out and be considered a real new genre, it can foresee the nude, sometimes even integral and explicit, but this is never an end in itself, everything that revolves around counts. naked in an artistic way, the way of posing one's body by posing and using glamorous techniques, feeling the story, feeling the situation and making it one's own by transmitting true emotions.
