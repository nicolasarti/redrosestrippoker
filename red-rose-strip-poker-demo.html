Red Rose Strip Poker Demo

Nicolas Bellearti
Red Rose Strip Poker the first Strip Poker themed video game designed to be a work of art.
100% open source and independent software.
Compatible with Windows and Linux PCs.
It was born with the idea of ​​providing a useful tool for photographers, videomakers and models to be able to express their art and if needed monetize by creating a customized Strip Poker themed video game, protecting their work and their privacy. This free, freely playable demo version that does not include underwear or nudity is inspired by old strip poker-themed video games from the 1980s and early 1990s, but the game engine is capable of playing clips and images up to 4k resolution.
