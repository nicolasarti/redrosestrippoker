Red Rose Strip Poker gives Real Emotions

Nicolas Bellearti
Red Rose Strip Poker is based on the open source Kiss Strip Game Engine project and fully embraces the philosophy with which this engine tries to provide a strip themed video game capable of giving emotions. The clips are organized according to the emotional situations described below and to have the maximum longevity for each type of situation multiple shots or clips are made which will then be proposed randomly during gameplay,making each game unique and unrepeatable:
 
CAR abbreviation of CARDS the situation in which the model starts a new hand of poker and then shuffles or distributes the playing cards
 
 
ACT the situation in which the model is intent on the normal course of the poker hand, generally with the five cards in hand, she will look at the cards with more or less fun, sexy or provocative or curious ... creativity has no limits
 
 
WIN the situation in which the model has won the hand, so she will generally show smiling, amused but also winking or making fun, or breathing a sigh of relief, the imagination has no limits as well as spontaneity
 
 
LOS abbreviation of LOSE, that is the situation in which the model has lost her hand in poker and therefore will generally show herself disappointed, angry, worried ... .. even here spontaneity and imagination are fundamental components
 
 
RIS abbreviation of RISK, the situation in which the model is playing a hand of poker full of tension because it is "risky" in the sense that in case of further defeat she will have to remove a garment. These clips should perhaps be the ones that convey the most evident and conflicting emotions, for example the model could convey the tension given by not wanting to absolutely lose this hand of poker and therefore she can also show her concern or embarrassment, perhaps taking time but why not he could also try to distract the opponent with winks or try to make him uncomfortable by showing himself extremely sure of herself, it all depends on how you want to play the part, on the attitude of the model; conflicting emotions are welcome. Obviously these emotions will be barely hinted at at the beginning and growing with the passage of the removed clothing.
 
 
OFF the situation in which the model has lost the poker hand and therefore she has to remove a garment. Generally the first clothes removed could be accessories (for example one or two jewels, the belt, hair tie, glasses etc.), the way to do it will be simply playful, but clearly with the continuation of the game things will change and here based on the attitude of the model, emphasis will be given, for example, to moving slowly, to slowly and carefully remove clothing or to undo the buttons of a garment slowly as if they were petals of a flower or even why not hesitating a few seconds in indecision which garment to remove or acting with anger or embarrassment or on the contrary provoking with maximum sensuality and later still using glamorous techniques such as hand bra rather than covering up with poker cards, thus asking to play an extra poker hand ... here all the art of the model and the photographer / video maker comes into play.
 
also there are two other types of clips that however do not concern the gameplay but simply the introduction scenes to the game and the final scenes:
 
ENT abbreviation of ENTER the situation in which the game begins the model shows up showing her outfit or walking around the location with a smile and why not a joke
 
END the situation in which the model has lost the game by remaining naked and therefore based on her attitude and art she will elegantly show all her beauty by posing freely.
